<p>Spear in hand, fire in eyes,<br>Son of Shiva, lightning skies.<br>Peacock dances, snakes bow low,<br>Warrior’s wrath, swift as arrow.</p><p>Six-faced, six mothers’ grace,<br>Yet none could tame his battle pace.<br>He beheaded Brahma’s pride,<br>Taught the gods where truth resides.</p><p>Call his name, just once, just true<br>He’ll charge through hell to reach you.<br>No grand rites, no gold, no plea,<br>Just stand firm; he’ll set you free.</p><p>Skanda rides, the earth shakes,<br>Dharma’s drum his heartbeat makes.<br>Fail or fall, he’ll lift you high,<br>Make him guru, he’ll set you right.</p><p><strong>Jai Canda Bhairava </strong></p>
