<p>No one owns Bhairava<br>He rises from Shiva’s third eye,<br>a flash against the pride of Brahma,<br>headless ego rolling in dust.</p><p>Not just rage; he is knowledge:<br>the Guru’s secret,<br>procedural collapse where all rules fail.<br>He stands at Kashi’s gate, holding keys to moksha,<br>burning karma faster than time can measure.</p><p>His vahana; a dog, loyal and simple<br>feed it and you feed Bhairava;<br>in every stray’s eyes his yantra glimmers.</p><p>Come as you are: raw, broken, unadorned.<br>Books won’t bring him closer; sincerity will.<br>Invoke him with an empty cup<br>when he chooses you, mountains move to you.</p><p>Jai Bhairava Baba</p><p>Om shri Gurubhyo Namah </p>
