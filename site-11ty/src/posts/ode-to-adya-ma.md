<p>Adya Ma, the ancient one,<br>Source of all, beyond the sun.<br>Goddesses arise from you<br>Kali, Sita, forms anew.</p><p>Before all worlds your presence shone,<br>Even gods know not where you're from.<br>Above Mahavidyas and Shakti Peethas’ domain,<br>Formless and with form, you reign.</p><p>To worship you is love’s true art<br>Pure devotion from the heart.<br>Surrender all; both joy and pain<br>In your embrace we break each chain.</p><p>Mother of creation’s flow,<br>From you all energies grow.<br>O Adya Ma, forever near<br>With bhakti pure we hold you dear. </p><p>Jai Ma Adya</p>
