<p>From churning darkness, a fiery birth,<br>Bhairava rises, a chaotic worth.<br>Not gentle solace, a soothing plea,<br>But raw intensity, eternally free.</p><p>He holds the storm within his gaze,<br>A restless spirit in a burning haze.<br>The Serpent’s Heart, a potent sign,<br>Of transformation, a truth divine.</p><p>He whispers warnings, sharp and deep,<br>Of paths untrodden, secrets to keep.<br>For those who chase the fleeting grace,<br>He’ll shatter ambition, leave not a trace.</p><p>He’s the destroyer, a forceful hand,<br>Yet offers solace, a helping stand.<br>For mistakes we make, a bitter cost,<br>He’ll forgive us all, forever lost</p><p>In the echoing depths of his domain,<br>A watchful guardian, easing pain.<br>He’s not a monster, fierce and grim,<br>But a source of strength, a sacred hymn.</p><p>So listen closely, to the serpent's call,<br>And let the power rise, encompassing all.<br>For Bhairava’s wisdom, burning bright,<br>Will illuminate the darkness, and set things right.</p><p></p><p>Jai Ma Adya , Jai Kalbhairav Baba</p>
