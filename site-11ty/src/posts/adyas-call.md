<p>Beyond the forms you seek, I stand<br>Adya, the first, the flame unplanned.<br>No shrine yet holds My true face,<br>No chant confines My boundless grace.</p><p>The lake where Kanda’s fire was spun,<br>The wounds of bloodlines left undone<br>I break the chains your fathers bore,<br>Yet fools still knock at temple doors.</p><p>Praise not the Peethas;I designed their fall,<br>While you beg scraps, I birthed them all.<br>Six sparks of Shiva lodged in Me,<br>Burned to a son none else could see.</p><p>Your grief? A thread I twist and tear,<br>Your lust? My jasmine scents the air.<br>Child, if shadows stalk your nights,<br>Know; even ghosts kneel to My rites.</p><p>Jai Ma Adya </p>
