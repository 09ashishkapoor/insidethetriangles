<p>Fearless Bhairava, dark and bright,<br>Shiva’s anger, Shiva’s light.<br>Dog beside you, fierce and true,<br>You protect the ones who kneel to you.</p><p>You cut the ego, proud and vain,<br>Yet you heal the deepest pain.<br>With Kaali’s love, you dance so free,<br>You are the path, the key to see.</p><p>Chant your name, just one time,<br>Feel your power, wild, divine.<br>No big prayers, no gold, no show<br>Just a heart that wants to know.</p><p>Bhairava, take my hand,<br>Burn my sins in firebrand.<br>Make me strong, make me wise,<br>Open wide my sleeping eyes.</p><p>Jai Bhairava</p>
