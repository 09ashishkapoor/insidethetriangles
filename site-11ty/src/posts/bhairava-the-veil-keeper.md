<p>Oh, fierce one, with eyes that flash like thunder,<br>A tempest in form, yet a gentle heart under.<br>A fulcrum of dawn and dusk, you are a Shepard of opposing sides.<br>In you, destruction and creation find their voice.<br>Where others see sin, you see only a choice made,<br>guiding orphans in this yuga of venom and vice.<br>Not fearing your might, for your heart is kind,<br>A Bholenath, fearless, free, and forever loving.<br>You move through time, a force both deep and wide,<br>Stabilizing energies, guiding seekers to stride.<br>With Shakti's power, you lead them to stillness,<br>Making the world's chaos seem trivial, its ills vanish.<br>In your embrace, the third eye ignites,<br>A tree of knowledge growing in the night.<br>You pull them close, transforming their essence,<br>A journey through fire and ice, unguessing.<br>So here's to you, Bhairava, both fierce and tender,<br>A force that guides, a light beyond all thunder.<br>In your wisdom, seekers find their path,<br>An alchemy of light and shadow, the ultimate path.</p><p>Om Adyaye Namah</p>
