<p>Adya Ma's fiery gaze comes down<br>
A transformative energy that knows no bound<br>
She cuts through my ego's tangled threads<br>
revealing thy true self, buried deep in unknown dread<br>
With each step, the haziness goes away<br>
Exposing the true darkness, black and grey<br>
The worldly attachments shatter like glass<br>
As Adya Ma's truth begins to amass<br>
In her dark embrace, the heart unfolds<br>
A labyrinth of self-doubt, where shadows grow old<br>
Through trials and tribulations, she guides<br>
Until the heart is remade, and Moksha abides<br>
Transmuted, I emerge, reborn anew<br>
A lotus unfurled, with heart pure and true.</p>
