<p>In the hush between thought and breath,<br>Where silence deepens beyond death,<br>There rises a shadow clothed in night<br>Adya Ma, primordial light.</p><p>Before Brahma’s head fell to ground,<br>Before mantras circled or drums made sound,<br>You danced alone in timeless space<br>The womb of cosmos, time and place.</p><p>Four Vedas sing Your endless praise;<br>The fifth Veda Bhairava lays.<br>Procedures crumble at Your feet;<br>In You, all destinies meet.</p><p>Not in gold nor silken thread<br>But ashes mark the path we tread.<br>Bloodline memories stir and burn;<br>To You alone our spirits turn.</p><p>You are the hunger that births desire,<br>The flame that purifies through fire.<br>You hold within both form and void<br>All worlds sustained, all forms destroyed.</p><p>O Mother clad in starless sky,<br>Kali fierce with flaming eye,<br>Who wears the skulls of time now dead<br>We place our longing at Your tread.</p><p>No temple holds Your boundless heart;<br>You move us whole and tear apart.<br>To know You is to lose all claim:<br>To die in ego, be reborn in Name.</p><p>So let this seeker be unmade;<br>Let rituals fall and roles all fade.<br>Only Your love remains<br>Adya Ma, I am become.</p><p>Jai Ma Adya </p>
