<p>Adya Ma, O Mother true,<br>You are the sky’s endless blue.<br>Before all gods and time began,<br>You held the world within Your hand.</p><p>You are the strength when I am weak,<br>The gentle voice I long to seek.<br>In joy and sorrow, night and day,<br>You guide my heart along its way.</p><p>No temple walls can hold Your light<br>You shine in darkness and in bright.<br>Mother of all, to You I pray:<br>Only Oneness no other play.</p><p>Jai Ma Adya </p>
